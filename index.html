Our Bahamian Projects

Nassau Financial Center

Downtown Nassau | Completed 2022

A 15-story Class A office tower featuring state-of-the-art amenities, hurricane-resistant design, and panoramic views of Nassau Harbour. This landmark building includes ground-floor retail space and underground parking.

Project Highlights:

  • 250,000 square feet of office space
  • Category 5 hurricane-rated structure
  • Completed during pandemic challenges
  • Advanced energy-efficient systems

Grand Exuma Resort & Spa

Great Exuma | Completed 2021

A 120-room luxury resort featuring overwater bungalows, spa facilities, and multiple dining venues. The project included extensive marine construction and environmental preservation measures to protect the surrounding ecosystem.

Project Highlights:

  • Sustainable design with solar power integration
  • Marine-sensitive construction techniques
  • Local artisan craftsmanship throughout
  • Completed during travel restrictions

Bahamas Medical Center

Freeport, Grand Bahama | Completed 2020

A 150,000 square foot medical complex with specialized hurricane-proof infrastructure for healthcare services. The facility includes emergency services, surgical suites, and diagnostic imaging, designed to remain operational during severe weather events.

Project Highlights:

  • Backup power for 7-day continuous operation
  • Water purification and storage systems
  • Built to post-Hurricane Dorian standards
  • Local workforce training program

Our Bahamian Story

Building The Bahamas Since 1998

Founded by Ervin Knowles with a small team of local craftsmen, EK Construction has grown from residential projects to become the premier commercial construction firm in The Bahamas.

What began as a family operation now employs over 150 skilled Bahamian professionals, including project managers, superintendents, and tradespeople who share our commitment to quality and community development.

While we've grown in capability, we've maintained our founder's commitment to building up The Bahamas while preserving our natural beauty and cultural heritage.

Our Timeline

1998 - Humble Beginnings

Ervin Knowles founds the company, completing small residential projects throughout New Providence.

2005 - First Major Project

Awarded contract for the Nassau Government Complex, our first multi-million dollar project, establishing our commercial construction expertise.

2012 - Family Leadership

Ervin's son Emile joins as CEO, bringing modern management practices while daughter Brittany leads operations.

2016 - Hurricane Resilience

Pioneered new hurricane-resistant construction techniques after Hurricane Matthew, becoming industry leaders in storm-proof design.

2020 - National Recognition

Received the Prime Minister's Award for Excellence in Construction for our work on the Bahamas Medical Center.
