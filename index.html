Our Featured Projects

Pacific Financial Center

Downtown Seattle | Completed 2022

A 25-story Class A office tower featuring state-of-the-art amenities, sustainable design elements, and panoramic views of Puget Sound. This LEED Platinum certified building includes a 5-story parking garage and ground-floor retail space.

Project Highlights:

  • 450,000 square feet of office space
  • LEED Platinum certification
  • Completed 3 weeks ahead of schedule
  • Advanced seismic safety features

Evergreen Medical Pavilion

Bellevue, WA | Completed 2021

A 120,000 square foot medical office building with specialized infrastructure for healthcare tenants. The facility includes outpatient surgery centers, imaging facilities, and a pharmacy, all designed with patient comfort and operational efficiency in mind.

Project Highlights:

  • Specialized MEP systems for medical use
  • Healing gardens and natural light throughout
  • Seamless phasing to maintain adjacent hospital operations
  • Energy-efficient building envelope

Cascade Retail Commons

Tacoma, WA | Completed 2020

A 300,000 square foot mixed-use retail development featuring big box anchors, specialty shops, restaurants, and entertainment venues. The project included extensive site work, utility infrastructure, and innovative stormwater management systems.

Project Highlights:

  • Complex phasing to maintain access for existing businesses
  • Custom facade treatments for each major tenant
  • Sustainable site development practices
  • Accelerated schedule to meet holiday season opening

Our Story

Building Excellence Since 1992

Founded by Robert Summit with just a pickup truck and a vision, Summit Construction has grown from small residential projects to become one of the most respected commercial construction firms in the Pacific Northwest.

What began as a three-person operation now employs over 200 skilled professionals, including project managers, superintendents, and tradespeople who share our commitment to quality and integrity.

While we've grown in size and capability, we've maintained our founder's commitment to treating every project—no matter the scale—with the care and attention it deserves.

Our Timeline

1992 - Humble Beginnings

Robert Summit founds the company, completing small residential and commercial projects throughout King County.

1998 - First Major Project

Awarded contract for the Bellevue Medical Plaza, our first multi-million dollar project, establishing our healthcare construction expertise.

2005 - Expansion

Opened our Portland office to serve the growing Oregon market, completing 12 projects in our first year.

2012 - Sustainability Focus

Launched our Green Building Division, completing our first LEED Platinum certified project the following year.

2020 - Next Generation

Robert's daughter, Emily Summit, becomes CEO, bringing innovative technologies and modern management practices to the company.
